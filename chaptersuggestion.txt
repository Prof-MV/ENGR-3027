  Suggested Priority Order for Development

  Tier 1 (Essential for all technicians):
  1. SPC — Used daily in all three industries
  2. TPM/Maintenance — Technicians are often responsible for equipment upkeep
  3. Industrial Automation — Core electromechanical content
  4. Troubleshooting/RCA — Practical problem-solving skills

  Tier 2 (Important for career growth):
  5. Quality Management Systems — Understanding certifications and audits
  6. MSA — Validates measurement systems technicians use
  7. Industrial Robotics — Growing in all industries

  Tier 3 (Industry differentiation):
  8. One industry-specific chapter based on your program's primary placement sector
